## Version A — Dev/Tech Group

### 🇬🇧 English

Hey guys, wanted to share a side project I just published — Xpnsio, a budgeting app.

The idea is different from most apps: instead of tracking expenses after the fact, you set a budget per category first (daily/weekly/monthly), then track against it. The dashboard shows what's left — not just what you've spent.

New in v0.2.0: color-coded progress bars (green/yellow/red) so you can see at a glance whether you're safe, in the caution zone, or over budget.

Stack: Next.js 15, Supabase, Clean Architecture. Built with Claude (AI) — but I set up a CLAUDE.md to enforce architecture rules so the AI stays within the right boundaries. AI-assisted, not AI-generated.

Currently free, no ads.
https://xpnsio.vercel.app?utm_source=whatsapp&utm_medium=chat

If anyone tries it, any feedback is welcome 🙏
