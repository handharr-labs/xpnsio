## Post Copy

I got tired of not knowing where my money went. So I built an app.

Most expense trackers tell you AFTER you've overspent.
That's too late.

Xpnsio flips the model:
→ Set a budget per category first
→ Track against it daily/weekly/monthly
→ Visual progress bars: green (safe), yellow (caution), red (overrun)
→ Always know what's LEFT — not just what you spent
→ Your data, your control — delete your account anytime

The difference sounds small. The behavioral effect isn't.

Stack: Next.js 15 · React 19 · Supabase · Drizzle ORM · Clean Architecture
Built with Claude (AI-assisted, not AI-generated — there's a difference)

Solo project. Currently free, no ads. Just looking for people who budget the same way I do.
